## Key Content Learnings
- Reels consistently reach 55-90%+ non-followers — algorithm distributing well
- Carousels are 100% followers — algorithm does NOT distribute carousels on small accounts
- Carousels underperform significantly vs reels — reels are the priority format
- Skip rate is the #1 metric to improve — currently 38-54% across reels
- **Shares are the strongest algorithm signal** — 18 shares on Morioka reel triggered Reels tab distribution at scale
- **Niche interest content with passionate communities outperforms broad travel content** — watch enthusiasts, fragrance collectors, football fans share aggressively within their networks
- Never use AI generated covers — always use own real photos
- Lead opening frame with most visually unusual/striking shot — not chronological order
- Food content drives saves, travel/story content drives comments
- Weekday posting outperforms weekends
- Tag real venues naturally in caption — they may reshare
- Hot take hook format works well ("Everyone says X, actually Y")
- Captions should NOT repeat what's already shown in the video
- Short, personal voice captions outperform descriptive listicle-style copy
- Soft CTAs (emoji drops, save prompts) work better than hard sales pitches
- "Nobody warns you about..." hook is overused — avoid
- Em dashes in captions read as AI-generated — avoid
- Designer brand bottles (Chanel, Dior, Prada) avoided on screen — copyright strike risk on Meta
- Niche fragrance houses (Creed, Amouage, MFK, Roja, Francesca Bianchi) safe and resonate better
- **Nostalgia/emotion angle works well with travel audience** — "Missing Japan" reel hit 93.1% non-followers in 6 hours
